Te Whare Tapa Whā

The four dimensions of well-being (My WB plan)

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

Eating healthy at least 80 percent of the week
Going on a walk in the sun daily during lunch breaks
Pilates 3x per week
30 minute home workout 2x per week

Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing)

Spending quality time with my partner 1x per week
Catch up with mum and walk doggies 1x per week
Message sisters/check up on nephew 1x per week


Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

Daily journal dump
Small guided meditation when feeling stressed
Stretches every night


Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

Make time to list 3 things I’m grateful for each day


Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)

Laying outside in the backyard on a nice day once a week
